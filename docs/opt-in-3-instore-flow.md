## Compliance Notes
- Customer verbally provides phone number (not assumed or looked up)
- Customer must tap "I Agree" on customer-facing display (active consent)
- Full consent language displayed before confirmation
- Consent explicitly states it's not required for purchase
- STOP/HELP instructions shown on all screens
- Msg & data rates disclosure included
- Receipt provides written proof of opt-in
- Confirmation SMS sent immediately
- Customer can decline at any point ("No Thanks" / "Skip Enrollment")
- Terms of Service URL provided for reference
- Dual confirmation: cashier checks consent box + customer taps confirm
