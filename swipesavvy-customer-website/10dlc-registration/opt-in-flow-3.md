# Opt-in Method 3: Point-of-Sale (POS) Customer Enrollment

## User Experience Flow (Step-by-Step)

**Step 1:** Customer makes a purchase at a participating merchant using Shop Savvy POS.

**Step 2:** Cashier asks: "Would you like to join our rewards program and save on future purchases?"

**Step 3:** Customer agrees. Cashier selects "Enroll New Customer" on POS terminal.

**Step 4:** Customer-facing display shows enrollment form:
- Name
- Phone Number
- Email (optional)

**Step 5:** After entering phone, SMS consent screen displays:

---

**Step 6:** Customer taps "Continue" (with SMS) or "Skip SMS" (without).

**Step 7:** Enrollment complete screen: "Welcome! You've earned [X] points on today's purchase."

**Step 8:** If SMS opted in, customer receives:
"Welcome to Swipe Savvy Rewards! You earned [X] pts today. Check balance anytime. Reply STOP to opt out, HELP for help. Msg&data rates apply."

**Step 9:** Receipt prints with rewards program details and opt-out instructions.
