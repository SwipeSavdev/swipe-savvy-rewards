# Opt-in Method 1: Website Contact Form

## User Experience Flow (Step-by-Step)

**Step 1:** User visits swipesavvy.com/contact to request a demo or sales inquiry.

**Step 2:** User fills out the contact form with required fields:
- First Name, Last Name
- Business Email
- Phone Number
- Company Name
- Industry (dropdown)
- Number of Locations (dropdown)

**Step 3:** User reaches the SMS Consent section which displays:

**Step 4:** User must check the SMS consent checkbox (required field) and Privacy/Terms checkbox.

**Step 5:** User clicks "Submit Request" button.

**Step 6:** Confirmation message displays: "Thank you! Your request has been submitted successfully. Our team will contact you within 24 hours."

"Welcome to Swipe Savvy! You've opted in to receive SMS updates. Reply STOP to unsubscribe, HELP for help. Msg&data rates apply."
