## Beta Tester Support Resources

### FAQ Document

```markdown
# BETA TESTER FAQ

## Getting Started

Q: I didn't receive my credentials. What should I do?
A: Check your spam folder. If still missing, email beta@swipesavvy.com

Q: How do I test the API if I'm not a developer?
A: Use our Quick Start guide with cURL examples. We also provide Postman collection.

Q: Can I use this with real bank accounts?
A: This is a sandbox environment with test data. No real transactions occur.

## Technical Issues

Q: I'm getting "Authentication failed" error.
A: Verify your API key is correct and included in the Authorization header.

Q: The API is responding slowly.
A: First request may take 2-3 seconds. Report if consistently slow.

Q: What if I encounter a bug?
A: Post in #bug-reports with: what you did, what happened, what you expected.

## Testing

Q: What should I test?
A: Everything! Try normal use, edge cases, and creative scenarios.

Q: How much testing is expected?
A: 2-5 hours per week. Quality over quantity.

Q: Should I test the same thing multiple times?
A: Yes! We want to verify consistency and reliability.

## Feedback

Q: How often should I provide feedback?
A: Weekly survey + ad-hoc feedback anytime via Slack or email.

Q: What makes good feedback?
A: Specific examples, screenshots, session IDs, and suggestions.

Q: Will my feedback be acted upon?
A: We review all feedback. Critical bugs fixed immediately, features evaluated for roadmap.

## Program Details

Q: What happens after the beta?
A: You get 6 months free premium subscription + early access to new features.

Q: Can I invite friends?
A: Not yet. Beta is limited to 30 testers. We'll expand later.

Q: What if I can't complete the full 4 weeks?
A: Let us know. We understand! Partial participation still valuable.
```
