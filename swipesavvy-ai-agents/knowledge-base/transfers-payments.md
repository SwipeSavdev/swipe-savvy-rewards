# Sending and Receiving Money with SwipeSavvy

## Peer-to-Peer (P2P) Transfers

### Send Money to Friends and Family

**Quick Send:**
1. Tap "Send Money" on home screen
2. Enter recipient:
   - Search by name (if previously sent)
   - Enter phone number or email
   - Scan SwipeSavvy QR code
3. Enter amount
4. Add message/note (optional)
5. Review and confirm
6. Authenticate with biometrics or PIN
7. Money sent instantly!

**Recipient Requirements:**
- Must have SwipeSavvy account, or
- Will receive invitation to claim money
- Unclaimed money returns after 30 days

### Request Money

**Send Payment Request:**
1. Tap "Request Money"
2. Select contact or enter phone/email
3. Enter amount
4. Add reason (e.g., "Dinner split", "Rent share")
5. Send request
6. Recipient gets notification
7. They approve and pay

**Manage Requests:**
- View pending requests in "Requests" tab
- Cancel anytime before payment
- Send reminders (max once per week)
- Auto-expire after 30 days

### Split Bills

**Split Evenly:**
1. Enter total bill amount
2. Select number of people
3. Choose who pays (select from contacts)
4. SwipeSavvy automatically calculates shares
5. Sends payment requests to each person

**Split Unevenly:**
1. Enter total amount
2. Add people
3. Manually assign amounts to each person
4. Add tax and tip (auto-calculated percentages)
5. Send customized requests

## Bank Transfers

### Transfer to External Bank Account

**One-Time Transfer:**
1. Tap "Transfer" > "To Bank Account"
2. Enter bank details:
   - Account number
   - Routing number
   - Account type (checking/savings)
3. Enter amount
4. Choose speed:
   - Standard (1-3 business days) - Free
   - Instant (minutes) - $0.25 fee
5. Confirm transfer

**Linked Bank Accounts:**
1. Settings > Linked Accounts > Add Bank
2. Login with bank credentials (uses Plaid)
3. Select accounts to link
4. Micro-deposit verification (2-3 days)
5. Verify amounts
6. Account linked!

**Transfer from Linked Bank:**
- Instant transfer from/to linked banks
- No fees for standard transfers
- Higher transfer limits
- View balances from both accounts

### Direct Deposit Setup

**Add SwipeSavvy for Paycheck:**
1. Get routing and account numbers:
   - Tap "Settings" > "Account Details"
   - Copy routing: 123456789
   - Copy account: Your unique 16-digit number
2. Provide to employer/HR
3. Set up direct deposit:
   - Full paycheck, or
   - Split between accounts (e.g., 80% SwipeSavvy, 20% savings)

**Early Direct Deposit:**
- Get paid up to 2 days early
- Automatic with direct deposit
- No fees, no action required
- Funds available as soon as employer submits

## Bill Pay

### One-Time Bill Payment

1. Tap "Pay Bills"
2. Select payee:
   - Search from 50,000+ billers
   - Or add custom (any company/person)
3. Enter account number with biller
4. Enter amount
5. Choose payment date
6. Confirm

**Payment Methods:**
- Electronic payment (instant-3 days)
- Paper check mailed (5-7 days)
- SwipeSavvy selects fastest method

### Recurring Bill Pay

**Set Up Auto-Pay:**
1. Pay Bills > Recurring
2. Select biller
3. Choose frequency:
   - Weekly, bi-weekly, monthly, quarterly, annually
   - Or set custom schedule
4. Enter amount (fixed or variable)
5. Set start date
6. Optional end date or "until I cancel"

**Variable Amount Bills:**
- For bills that change (utilities, credit card)
- Receive notification with amount 3 days before due
- Approve or modify amount
- Auto-pays if not modified

### Manage Scheduled Payments

**View Upcoming:**
- See all scheduled payments in calendar view
- Filters by date, biller, status
- Mark as paid externally (to track)

**Edit or Cancel:**
- Edit payment up to 2 business days before scheduled date
- Cancel anytime before processing
- Changes confirmed via notification

## International Transfers

### Send Money Abroad

**Supported Countries:**
- 80+ countries worldwide
- Major currencies: EUR, GBP, CAD, MXN, INR, PHP, and more

**How to Send:**
1. Tap "Send Money" > "International"
2. Select country
3. Enter amount (USD)
4. See exchange rate and fees
5. Enter recipient details:
   - Full name
   - Bank account or mobile wallet
   - Required ID/info (varies by country)
6. Review total cost
7. Confirm transfer

**Transfer Speed:**
- Most countries: 1-3 business days
- Select countries: Same day or instant
- Tracking provided

**Fees:**
- Small fixed fee ($2.99-$9.99) depending on country
- Competitive exchange rates
- No hidden fees
- Cheaper than Western Union or MoneyGram

## Security

### Transfer Security

**Verification:**
- Biometric or PIN required for all transfers
- 2FA for large amounts (>$500)
- Confirm recipient details before sending

**Fraud Protection:**
- Real-time fraud monitoring
- Unusual transfers blocked
- Verify suspicious transfers via phone call
- Scam warnings for common scams

**Can't Reverse Transfers:**
- P2P transfers are instant and final
- Double-check recipient before sending
- Report fraud immediately
- May recover if recipient hasn't claimed

### Protect Yourself

**Red Flags:**
- ❌ Someone you don't know requests money
- ❌ "Emergency" requiring immediate transfer
- ❌ Payment for goods/services you haven't received
- ❌ Requests to send money to claim prize/refund
- ❌ Romance scam (met online, never in person)

**Safe Practices:**
- ✅ Only send to people you know and trust
- ✅ Use bill pay for businesses (has protections)
- ✅ Verify recipient via separate communication
- ✅ Never send money for unsolicited requests
- ✅ If it sounds too good to be true, it is

## Troubleshooting

### Transfer Pending

**Why Transfers Pend:**
- First time sending to recipient
- Large or unusual amount
- Recipient hasn't accepted yet
- Bank verification in progress
- Scheduled for future date

**What to Do:**
- Check status in "Activity" tab
- Estimated completion date shown
- Cancel if needed (before processing)
- Contact support if delayed >24 hours

### Transfer Failed

**Common Reasons:**
- Insufficient funds
- Daily/monthly limit exceeded
- Incorrect recipient details
- Bank account closed
- Blocked for security

**How to Fix:**
- Check error message for specific reason
- Verify recipient information
- Ensure sufficient balance
- Contact support for security blocks
- Try again after fixing issue

### Money Not Received

**Recipient Troubleshooting:**
- Check spam/junk for notification email
- Look in pending transfers
- May need to create SwipeSavvy account to claim
- Money held for 30 days before refund
- Contact sender to verify amount and details
