# How to Check Your Account Balance

## Quick Access

Your account balance is always visible on the SwipeSavvy home screen when you open the app. For security, it may be hidden with asterisks (****) by default.

### Steps to View Balance:

1. **Open SwipeSavvy App**
   - Launch the app from your device
   - Authenticate with Face ID, Touch ID, or PIN

2. **Home Screen Display**
   - Balance shown at the top of the screen
   - Tap the eye icon to reveal/hide balance
   - Pull down to refresh for real-time updates

3. **Detailed View**
   - Tap "Accounts" tab at the bottom
   - See breakdown by account type
   - View available vs. pending balances

## Alternative Methods

### Home Screen Widget
- Add SwipeSavvy widget to your phone home screen
- Shows balance without opening app
- Updates automatically every 30 minutes
- Requires device unlock to view

### Voice Assistant
- **iOS (Siri):** "Hey Siri, what's my SwipeSavvy balance?"
- **Android (Google):** "OK Google, check my SwipeSavvy balance"
- Requires voice authentication setup

### Smart Watch
- View balance on Apple Watch or Wear OS
- Swipe to SwipeSavvy complications
- Tap for quick transactions

## Balance Types

### Available Balance
The amount you can spend or withdraw immediately. This accounts for:
- Cleared deposits
- Posted transactions
- Pending authorizations (deducted)
- Holds (deducted)

### Current Balance  
Your account balance including:
- All cleared transactions
- Pending deposits (not yet available)
- May be higher than available balance

### Pending Balance
Shows transactions that are:
- Authorized but not yet posted
- Processing (1-3 business days)
- Debit card holds from gas stations, hotels

## Security Features

### Hidden by Default
- Balance hidden with asterisks for privacy
- Prevents shoulder surfing in public
- Customize in Settings > Privacy > Hide Balance

### Biometric Protection
- Requires Face ID/Touch ID to view
- Automatic hide after 30 seconds
- Re-authentication for each app open

### Notifications
- Optional balance change alerts
- Daily balance summary notifications
- Low balance warnings (customizable threshold)

## Troubleshooting

### Balance Not Updating
1. Pull down to refresh manually
2. Check internet connection
3. Log out and log back in
4. Clear app cache (Settings > Storage)

### Balance Looks Wrong
- Check for pending transactions
- Review recent transactions for errors
- Wait 10 minutes for system sync
- Contact support if discrepancy persists

### Can't See Balance
- Ensure you're logged into correct account
- Check if parental controls are active
- Verify account is not frozen/suspended
- Update app to latest version
