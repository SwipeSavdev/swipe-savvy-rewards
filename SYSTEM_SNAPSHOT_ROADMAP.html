‚ö†Ô∏è Known Limitations & Future Improvements

Current Limitations

AI Concierge Context Window

Currently limited to last 20 messages in conversation. Conversation history older than that is not available to LLM.

Fix planned: Q2 2026 - Vector embeddings for semantic search across full history

Rewards Margin Risk Monitoring

Currently calculated daily. No real-time adjustments to spending multipliers.

Fix planned: Q1 2026 - Real-time margin monitoring with dynamic multiplier adjustment

Campaign Personalization

Limited to basic segmentation. No ML-based predictive targeting.

Fix planned: Q2 2026 - Propensity modeling and lookalike audiences

Mobile App Offline Sync

Some features not available offline. Manual refresh required on reconnect.

Fix planned: Q1 2026 - Automatic background sync with conflict resolution
