15 Must-Have Restaurant POS Features That Increase Revenue

From tableside ordering to kitchen display systems, these features can reduce ticket times by 35% and increase average order value by 22%.

Published: December 28, 20259 min read

The right restaurant POS system does more than process payments—it becomes the central nervous system of your operation, connecting front-of-house, kitchen, and back office in real-time.

After analyzing hundreds of restaurant implementations, we've identified the 15 features that deliver the highest ROI. Some are obvious must-haves; others are overlooked capabilities that can transform your operation.

Speed & Efficiency Features

📱 1. Tableside Ordering

Servers take orders directly at the table on tablets, sending them instantly to the kitchen. Eliminates trips to the terminal and transcription errors.

Impact: 15-20% faster table turns, 30% fewer order errors

📺 2. Kitchen Display System (KDS)

Replace paper tickets with digital screens. Orders auto-prioritize, color-code by timing, and track fulfillment in real-time.

Impact: 35% reduction in ticket times, better coordination

⚡ 3. Quick Service Mode

Streamlined interface for high-volume, fast-paced ordering. One-touch items, combo builders, and speed screens.

Impact: 40% faster order entry in QSR environments

💳 4. Contactless & Mobile Payments

Apple Pay, Google Pay, tap-to-pay cards. Faster checkout, no card swiping or dipping.

Impact: 15-30 seconds saved per transaction

Revenue-Boosting Features

🍔 5. Modifier Prompts & Upselling

Automatic prompts for add-ons, upgrades, and sides. "Would you like to add bacon?" becomes systematic, not random.

Impact: 12-22% increase in average ticket

💰 6. Combo/Bundle Builder

Easy meal deal creation and automatic pricing. Encourages larger orders while providing perceived value.

Impact: 18% higher average order value

🎁 7. Integrated Loyalty Program

Points, rewards, and visit tracking built into checkout. No separate cards or apps required.

Impact: 67% higher purchase frequency from loyalty members

"Our average check went from $14.50 to $17.80 just by enabling modifier prompts. That's $165,000 in extra annual revenue for our location."

Operations & Management Features

📦 8. Real-Time Inventory Tracking

Automatic inventory deduction as items sell. Track food costs, identify waste, set low-stock alerts.

Impact: 2-4% food cost reduction

👥 9. Employee Management & Permissions

Clock in/out, break tracking, role-based access, tip management. All in one system.

Impact: 5+ hours/week saved on scheduling admin

📊 10. Real-Time Sales Dashboard

Live view of sales, labor, and performance from anywhere. Mobile access for owners on the go.

Impact: Faster decision-making, problems caught early

🔒 11. Offline Mode

Keep taking orders and payments even when internet goes down. Syncs when connection returns.

Impact: Zero lost sales during outages

Guest Experience Features

📱 12. QR Code Ordering

Guests scan, browse menu, order, and pay from their phones. Reduces wait times, increases throughput.

Impact: 25% faster table turns, higher guest satisfaction

📩 13. Digital Receipts

Email or text receipts directly to guests. Captures contact info for marketing, reduces paper waste.

Impact: 40%+ email capture rate, better remarketing

🚚 14. Delivery Integration

DoorDash, UberEats, Grubhub orders flow directly into your POS. No tablet juggling or manual entry.

Impact: 90% reduction in delivery order errors

📅 15. Reservation & Waitlist Management

Built-in or integrated table management. Text guests when their table is ready.

Impact: Better floor utilization, reduced walk-aways

Quick ROI Calculator

A 500-cover/day restaurant that improves average ticket by just $2 generates an extra $365,000/year in revenue. Most POS upgrades pay for themselves in 2-3 months.

Features by Restaurant Type

  • Drive-thru integration

  • Mobile ordering integration

Fast Casual

  • QR code ordering
  • Text-when-ready alerts
  • Kiosk ordering option
  • Delivery integration

Full Service

  • Tableside ordering tablets
  • Course firing and pacing
  • Split checks and party management
  • Reservation integration

Bars & Nightclubs

  • Tab management
  • Pre-authorization holds
  • Age verification prompts
  • Happy hour automatic pricing

Implementation Tips

  1. Start with speed features: KDS and tableside ordering deliver fastest ROI
  2. Enable upselling immediately: Free revenue lift from day one
  3. Train staff thoroughly: Features only work if staff use them
  4. Track metrics before/after: Measure ticket times, average check, table turns
  5. Iterate based on data: Adjust modifier prompts and combos based on what sells
