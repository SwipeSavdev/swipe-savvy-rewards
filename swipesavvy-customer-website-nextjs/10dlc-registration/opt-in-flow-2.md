# Opt-in Method 2: Mobile App Registration

## User Experience Flow (Step-by-Step)

**Step 1:** User downloads Swipe Savvy Rewards app from App Store or Google Play.

**Step 2:** User taps "Create Account" on the welcome screen.

**Step 3:** User enters registration details:
- Full Name
- Email Address
- Mobile Phone Number
- Create Password

**Step 4:** SMS Consent screen appears with the following text:

---

**Stay Connected**

Enable SMS notifications to receive:
- Reward balance updates
- Exclusive offers & promotions
- Transaction confirmations
- Account security alerts

By continuing, you agree to our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

---

**Step 5:** User toggles SMS consent ON (optional but encouraged) and taps "Continue".

**Step 6:** Phone verification screen: "Enter the 6-digit code sent to [phone number]"

**Step 7:** User enters verification code received via SMS.

**Step 8:** Success screen: "Welcome to Swipe Savvy! Your account is ready. Start earning rewards today."

**Step 9:** If SMS opted in, user receives welcome message:
"Welcome to Swipe Savvy Rewards! Your account is active. Reply STOP to opt out, HELP for help. Msg&data rates apply."
