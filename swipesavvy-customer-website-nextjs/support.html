Self‑Service

Popular help topics

Choose a topic or ask Savvy AI (widget bottom‑right) for a guided answer.
Checkout
Fast answers for in‑store workflows.
  • Split payments, tips, discounts, and price overrides
  • Digital + printed receipts (email/SMS)
  • Refunds, exchanges, voids, and audit trails
  • Offline-first checkout (no internet)
Inventory
Ledger-based accuracy and controls.
  • Receiving, transfers, cycle counts, shrink tracking
  • Multi-location visibility (on-hand/reserved/ATP)
  • Low-stock alerts and replenishment rules
  • Perishable tracking (lot/batch/expiration) where applicable
Devices & Terminals
Connect and troubleshoot certified hardware.
  • Dejavoo and other certified payment terminals
  • Barcode scanners, receipt/kitchen printers, cash drawers
  • Centralized provisioning, monitoring, diagnostics
Reporting & AI
Dashboards, exports, and insights.
  • Sales/tax/discount/tender-mix reporting in real time
  • Inventory valuation, aging, shrink analytics
  • AI anomaly detection (shrink/fraud/performance drops)
  • Campaign performance + cohort insights
